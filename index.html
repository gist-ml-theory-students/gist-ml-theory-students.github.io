Welcome to GIST ML theory student group's webpage!

We aim to explore various aspects of machine learning and deep learning, while fostering a supportive community for GIST students (graduate or undergraduate) who are working on or interested in these areas.

Any questions are welcomed to gist.ml.theory@gmail.com.
